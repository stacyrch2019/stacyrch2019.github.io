About Us

The Fort Bookstore is an independent online bookstore built for readers who care about where their books come from. We believe that buying a book is more than a transaction — it's a choice to support independent bookselling, authors, and the stories that matter.

Through our partnership with Bookshop.org, we offer a carefully curated selection of titles alongside access to Ingram's vast catalog — one of the world's largest book distributors. Whether you're looking for a bestseller, a hard-to-find indie title, or something off the beaten path, The Fort Bookstore can source it for you.

What We Offer

Curated titles: Hand-selected books across fiction, nonfiction, and beyond — chosen with care and intention.

Ingram catalog access: Through Ingram's extensive distribution network, we can source thousands of titles including indie press books, small publisher releases, and hard-to-find reads.

Special orders: Can't find what you're looking for? We can source it. Our access to Ingram's catalog means an enormous range of titles is within reach.

Support indie bookselling: Every purchase through The Fort Bookstore supports independent bookselling and helps sustain a literary community outside the big-box ecosystem.
